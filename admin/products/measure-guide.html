Home › Products › Measuring guide
  • Always measure the item flat across, never the circumference.
  • Gently smooth out wrinkles, folds and creases, being careful not to stretch the fabric or distort the item's shape.
  • Lay the item flat on an even surface.
  • Measurements defined as "REQUIRED" have to be provided in order to illustrate the fit. Required measurements are indicated in bold print.
  • Optional measurements provide a more detailed picture of the fit for your customers, and so decrease the risk of returns. They can be added individually.
  • Either metal or plastic tape measures can be used, but be aware that plastic tape measures can stretch over time and should be checked periodically against a metal ruler to ensure accuracy.

Guide for Sleeve dress
Required measurements
200 Length.Measure straight down from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom of the dress (including any trim at the bottom).
102 Bust.Measure straight across, 2.5 cm (1 in.) below the bottom of the sleeve opening.
103 Waist.Measure straight across the narrowest part of the dress. If the dress doesn't have a defined waist, measure across 40 cm (16 in.) below the shoulder.
105 Sleeve.Measure along the top of the sleeve from the shoulder seam to the cuff edge.
Optional measurements
107 Hip.Measure straight across the front of the dress at the widest point below the waist, usually 18 cm (7 in.) below the waist.
111 Hem.Measure straight across the bottom of the dress.
206 Waist height.Measure straight down from the highest point on the shoulder to the narrowest part of the dress.
201 Bicep.Measure from where the bottom of the sleeve meets the body to the top of the sleeve, parallel to the cuff.
